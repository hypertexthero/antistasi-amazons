# Antistasi Amazons

**Version 0.0.1** - 12th June 2020 (Antistasi Amazons based on Antistasi Community Edition 2.2.1)

**Antistasi Amazons** Arma 3 video game modification based on the work of Barbolani, the Antistasi Community Edition Team, Maxjoiner, Zeealex, and Bohemia Interactive by Hypertexthero.

=============  Changes  ======================

  - Women guerrillas using [FEMAL3_Heads](https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=374775446) and [Max_Women](https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=145175).
  - New briefing and overview text.
  - New flag (flag.jpg - 200x200px), introduction card (pic.jpg - 2048x1024px), and map board card (mapboard.jpg called from init.sqf - 1024x1024px), with hand-drawn illustrations.
  - Disabled stamina.
  - Starting parameter defaults edited to enable fast travel, reduce the number of weapons required for unlocks to 10, and make crate contents random.
  - Added a basic rifle and removed one submachine gun from starting weapons.

=============  Issues  ======================

  - The hair of some of the female models in the Max_women and FEMAL3_Heads mods clip through hats and other head gear.
  - When you take temporary control of an AI guerrilla and relinquish it after 60 seconds the AI character becomes your own avatar.
